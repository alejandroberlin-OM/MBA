The Firm

Law shaped around your business

Misraji & Béjar is a boutique law firm specializing in business law. We build tailored legal solutions for each client, not standard formulas.

Founded by two senior partners with deep roots in Chile's most prestigious legal and financial institutions, the firm combines technical rigor with a partner-led model that guarantees direct, senior-level attention at every stage.

From cross-border M&A to tax structuring, real estate, and regulatory compliance, the firm works at the intersection of law and business strategy, serving entrepreneurs, family offices, and corporations across Latin America.
